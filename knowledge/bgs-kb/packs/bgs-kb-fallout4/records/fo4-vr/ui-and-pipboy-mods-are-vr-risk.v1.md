# UI and Pip-Boy mods are high-risk in Fallout 4 VR

Fallout 4 VR reworks core interaction and presentation for virtual reality.
Flat UI assumptions can fail when menus, V.A.T.S., Pip-Boy display, or workshop building are presented through VR controls.

Treat UI and interaction mods as VR-specific unless tested in Fallout 4 VR.
Data-only armor, weapon, or settlement mods are a different risk class than interface replacements.
