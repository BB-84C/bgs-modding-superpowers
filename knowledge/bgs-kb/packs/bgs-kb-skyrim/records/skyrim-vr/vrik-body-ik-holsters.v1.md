# VRIK supplies the visible Skyrim VR player body and holster framework

VRIK is not a cosmetic flat-screen mod.
It is a VR player-avatar and IK framework that makes the player body match VR movement.

Its page also documents holsters and gesture bindings, with SKSEVR and SkyUI VR requirements.
That makes it part of the core VR interaction stack for many modlists.

Configure and calibrate VRIK after the VR UI and skeleton/body stack are stable.
