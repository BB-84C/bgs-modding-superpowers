# Starfield Papyrus support claims require primary CK or runtime evidence

The probed Starfield Wiki Papyrus page had no substantive article text.
That does not prove Papyrus is absent; it proves this page is not usable evidence.

For Starfield scripting records, require a primary CK, SFSE, or runtime source.
Do not carry over Skyrim or Fallout 4 Papyrus behavior just because the engine lineage is familiar.
