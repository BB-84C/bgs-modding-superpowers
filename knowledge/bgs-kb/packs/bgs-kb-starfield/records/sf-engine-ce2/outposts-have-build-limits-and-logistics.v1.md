# Starfield outposts combine build limits, extraction, crafting, and logistics

Outposts are not just decorative player homes.
Starfield Wiki describes them as bases for resource extraction, crafting automation, storage, logistics, crew, power, and production.

Outposts also have build limits.
When diagnosing outpost mods, consider production/logistics state and object budgets, not just placed static records.
