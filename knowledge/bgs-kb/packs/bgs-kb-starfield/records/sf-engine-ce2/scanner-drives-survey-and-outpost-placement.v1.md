# Starfield scanner supports surveying and outpost placement workflows

The scanner identifies flora and fauna and reveals survey details as scans accumulate.
Starfield Wiki also describes outpost creation as beginning with an Outpost Beacon from the scanner HUD.

For mod triage, scanner UI and scanner-driven outpost setup are Starfield-specific surfaces.
Do not port Pip-Boy assumptions from Fallout 4 onto this workflow.
