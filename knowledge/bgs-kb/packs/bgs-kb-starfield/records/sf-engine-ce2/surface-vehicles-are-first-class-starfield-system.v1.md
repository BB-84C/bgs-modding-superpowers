# Surface vehicles are a Starfield-specific traversal system

Starfield Wiki describes vehicles used to move quickly across world surfaces.
Access and acquisition are part of Starfield's ship-services and Creation-era content surfaces.

Treat vehicle mods as Starfield traversal/content mods.
Do not infer compatibility from Skyrim mounts or Fallout 4 power armor frameworks.
