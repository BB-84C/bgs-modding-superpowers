# Starfield modding evidence is thinner than Skyrim and Fallout 4 evidence

Starfield has an active wiki and official Creations surface, but it does not yet have Skyrim's decade-plus documentation density.
Missing pages during this pass included Starfield Wiki entries for Creation Kit, Plugins, Data Files, and Papyrus.

Treat gaps as gaps.
Do not fill them with FO4 or Skyrim priors unless the record says it is an extrapolation.
